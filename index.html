Honest answers

The questions everyone asks

Mahal ba magpa-solar?
Not always. Many homeowners switch to solar for less than what they're already paying monthly for electricity. We'll compute your savings and recommend the right system — no pressure, no obligation.
Worth it ba talaga ang solar?
If you're paying high electric bills every month, solar can help you save long-term while giving you more energy independence and protection from rising electricity costs.
Gagana ba kahit maulan o walang araw?
Yes. Solar still produces power even on cloudy days, just at lower output. Your system is designed to account for weather conditions so your home stays powered efficiently year-round.
Gaano katagal ang installation?
Most residential systems are installed in just a few days. We handle the survey, planning, installation, and paperwork so the process stays simple for you.
Paano kung maliit lang ang bubong ko?
No problem. We design systems around your available roof space and energy needs — maximizing performance without overbuilding.
May ilaw pa ba kapag brownout?
It depends on your setup. Systems with battery backup can keep essential appliances running during outages. We'll help you choose the right setup for your needs.
Gaano kalaki ang matitipid ko sa kuryente?
Every home is different, but many families see significant savings on their monthly electric bill. We'll provide a personalized estimate based on your actual consumption.
